VanLegit

A respectful overnight vehicle legality planner for trip planning. Built to help travelers understand local restrictions, not challenge them.

Toolbelt planning · Works offline with Colorado priority ZIPs, GPS, or coordinates
Life is short. Camp where camping belongs. In a big world with a lot of campsites, there is no reason to overnight somewhere that clearly is not meant for it. VanLegit is about showing respect for local communities while helping travelers plan better.
Offline planning estimate only. Local ordinances change. Always verify current local rules and posted signage.
